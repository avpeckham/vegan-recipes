Beans and Chard

4 to 8 small cloves of garlic, minced or garlic-pressed
15 oz. can of tomatoes
15 oz. can of white beans
1 lb. of chard, chopped or torn into 1 1/2 inch pieces
1 cup stock (I use Rapunzel brand vegan stock)
Olive oil: enough to cover the garlic

1. On medium heat, saute the garlic in the oil for 30 seconds.

2. Put the chard on top. Wait a moment for it to wilt.

3. Add the beans, stock, and tomatoes and stir together.

4. Simmer till tender, about 5 minutes. Remove from heat and serve.
