Potato Chips
============

Gear
----

A large mixing bowl
A baking sheet
A large plate
A drainer
Foil
Paper towels

Ingredients
-----------

3 tablespoons olive oil, plus more to grease the baking sheet
3 potatoes
1 to 2 teaspoons salt
Optional: hot sauce

Steps
-----

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit.

2. Spray, brush, or spread the baking sheet lightly with olive oil.

3. Put a couple of paper towels on the plate.

4. Slice each potato perpendicular to its major axis. Make the slices
about 1/8" thick.

5. Put the slices in the first mixing bowl and cover them with cold water.
Leave them to soak for 15 minutes.

6. Using the drainer, drain the slices.

7. Pat the slices dry with a paper towel.

8. Put the slices on the paper towel-covered plate.

9. Dry the mixing bowl, then toss the slices with the 3 tablespoons of olive
oil, the salt, and optionally the hot sauce.

10. Bake at 400 degrees Fahrenheit for 15 minutes.

11. Remove, sprinkle with salt, and leave to cool.
